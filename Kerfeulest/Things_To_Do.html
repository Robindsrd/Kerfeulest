Things To Do


At Lowland Lodge there are many fun activities.
From a quiet hike in the hills to extreme water rafting,
we're sure there's something for everyone.


Fishing & Watersports

Only a short drive away, you can experience water skiing and jetboating on Lake Karapiro. If you're more into fishing, grab your fishing rod and fish off the rocks at Raglan. You can also cool the kids off at Waterworld or enjoy a picnic lunch at Hamilton Lake.

Hiking & Trails

Stretch your legs with a scenic walk around the Taitua Aboretum. If you're fitness buff, tackle the steps of the Hakarimata Walkway. A leisurely stroll around the ever expanding Hamilton Gardens will be great for the body and mind.

Adventure Park

If excitement is what you're after, Hamiltons' Adventure Park is the place to be. With activities ranging from ziplines to climbing walls to treetop walks. From young to old, there is something for everyone.

Winter Activities

Just a short drive from your lodge, you'll find everything you need for a day on the slopes. We hire skis, snowboards and include a free complementary one hour lesson with a qualified instructor. You can also try your hand at winter fishing for trout at a nearby lake.
