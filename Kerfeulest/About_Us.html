A propos de nous


Lowland Lodges is a family owned Lodge business that has been operating for over 30 years. We used to travel a lot as a family and all the lodges we stayed at were missing something in common: they didn't feel like a home away from home.
We started this business to change that. Each of our fully furnished lodges will make you feel at home as soon as you step inside. We set out to design the lodge my family and I would want to stay in - and I believe we've done just that.
